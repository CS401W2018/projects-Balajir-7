Title: Voyage Ventures

Topic:

A simple travel guide that gives information about the best tourist places in the world.

Why This Topic?

I chose this topic because many people like to travel but do not know where to go. I even saw in most FB group that people asking for suggeition of best touriest places and looking for tavel partner. So, my project is i am going to create a website that will help travelers find good places, see maps, and learn about hotels and find commen interest travel partner and more.

Website Content:

Homepage:

  • Short introduction about the website
  • Search bar to choose a country
  • Popular travel places
  • Links to different pages (Countries/ Popular travel places)

Page 1: Country List & Destination Highlights

  • List of all available countries with clickable links
  • Top tourist attractions for each country

Page 2: Destination Details

  • Short descriptions of each place
  • Pictures
  • Live map to see the location

Page 3: Hotels & Accommodations

  • List of hotels near the tourist places
  • Hotel prices and services
  • Links to book a hotel

Page 4: User Reviews & Testimonials

  • Form to write reviews
  • List of reviews and ratings

Page 5: Travel Statistics (Table)

  • Number of tourists visiting each country
  • Best time to visit
  • Average travel cost per day

Informations:

Form Fields:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Country of Residence
  • Preferred Destination
  • Review Message
  • Star Rating (1-5)

Table Data:

  • Country Name
  • Number of Visitors Per Year
  • Best Time to Visit
  • Average Travel Cost per Day (USD)
