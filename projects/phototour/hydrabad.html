HYDERBAD

Place in hyderbad

Hyderabad is the capital of southern India's Telangana state. A city for the technology industry, it's home to many growing restaurants and shops. Some historic sites are Golconda Fort, a former diamond-trading center. The Charminar, is an old city landmark near the long-standing Laad Bazaar.
